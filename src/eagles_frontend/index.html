Our Departments

Our medical departments provide specialized care across various health needs. From pediatrics to cardiology, our experienced professionals deliver personalized, compassionate treatment, ensuring high-quality healthcare in a supportive environment for all patients.

Cardiology

Our cardiology team specializes in diagnosing and treating heart-related conditions. We offer comprehensive care, from preventive heart screenings to advanced treatments for cardiovascular diseases.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedics specialists focus on treating musculoskeletal issues, including joint pain, fractures, and sports injuries. We provide both surgical and non-surgical options to restore mobility and improve quality of life.

Emergency Care Department

Our emergency department operates 24/7 to provide immediate medical attention. We handle critical situations, from trauma care to life-saving procedures, ensuring rapid response and expert care when every second counts.

First Aid

Our First Aid department offers immediate care for injuries and emergencies. Our trained professionals provide essential support, ensuring quick assessments and treatments to stabilize patients before further medical assistance is available.
